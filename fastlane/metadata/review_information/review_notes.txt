即做 DoNow is a local task/errand platform for Hong Kong.

Key features:
- Users can post tasks/jobs they need help with
- Workers can browse and accept tasks
- In-app messaging for communication
- Wallet system for payments (top-up and withdrawal)
- ID verification for safety

Age restriction: 16+
Platform fee: 10% from both poster and worker

Data protection: All data stored in Hong Kong (asia-east2) for PDPO compliance.

Payment processing: Stripe (real money transactions)

This app does not contain:
- User-generated content without moderation
- Gambling or contests
- Medical/health advice
- Financial services beyond payment processing
